Scene 1:
The water on Earth is millions of years old. It is constantly recycling in a loop called the water cycle.

Scene 2:
First, the hot sun warms the water in oceans and lakes. The water turns into invisible steam and rises.

Scene 3:
High in the cold sky, this steam cools down and gathers together to form soft white clouds.

Scene 4:
When clouds get too heavy, they release the water as rain or snow, returning water back to the Earth.
