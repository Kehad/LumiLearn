Scene 1:
All living things need food to grow. While animals find food, plants make their own using sunlight.

Scene 2:
Inside plant leaves, there is a green pigment called chlorophyll. It acts like a solar panel to capture light.

Scene 3:
Plants also absorb water from the soil through roots, and pull in carbon dioxide gas from the air.

Scene 4:
Sunlight splits water and gas into glucose sugar for food, and releases clean oxygen for us to breathe.
